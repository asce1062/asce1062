LOCAL STORAGE

This site writes six values to your browser's localStorage:

- "theme": "light" or "dark" - your chosen color scheme.
- "match-device-theme": "1" when the "Match device" sidebar toggle is
  enabled; absent otherwise. When active, the site theme follows your
  OS/browser color scheme preference live.
- "cursor-blink": "paused" when you have disabled the blinking cursor
  effect; absent otherwise.
- "stars-bg": "1" when the animated stars background is enabled via the
  "Stars" sidebar toggle; absent otherwise.
- "matrix-bg": "1" when the Matrix rain canvas background is enabled via
  the "Matrix" sidebar toggle; absent otherwise. Mutually exclusive with
  stars-bg (enabling one disables the other).
- "avatar-state": your 8-bit avatar customisation (e.g.
  "gender=male&avatar=3-54-12-14-15-21"). Only written if you use the
  avatar builder at /8biticon; absent until you interact with it.

All six values persist across browser restarts by design so your
preferences are remembered between visits. They never leave your device
and are never transmitted to a server.

FORMS
