The Gift of Hindsight

2025-06-23 | Growth often hides in the moments we wish we could rewrite. But maybe we were never off course tags: motivation
  • If I had the mind I have now, I wouldn’t have made the mistakes I made back then
  • But if I hadn’t made the mistakes I made back then, I wouldn’t have the mind I have now
  • It’s easy to look back and feel regret, to wish we had done things differently
  • But the truth is, everything happened the way it was meant to
  • Every wrong turn, every setback, every choice. It all led us here
  • Maybe here is exactly where we are supposed to be1
  • So; do not hold on to the past, make room for what is coming next
