Love Doesn’t Excuse; It Liberates

2025-07-28 | Forgiveness doesn't mean forgetting; it means refusing to let pain define who you become tags: motivation
  • You can’t have a heart that’s hateless, but you can have a heart that hates less
  • Hate is easy, but it drains you slowly, it robs your peace. It poisons you more than it punishes them
  • Love frees you. It doesn’t mean forgetting, pretending it didn’t hurt
  • It means refusing to let the pain turn you into someone you are not
  • A heart that hates less makes room for peace and peace is worth protecting1
  • You can’t walk in love while dragging hate behind you
  • Sooner or later, one has to let go
